Value Engineering is a continuous review and refinement process of design and specification, which augments the design efforts from initial concept up to the issuance of the last construction variation. It is normal that the integration of a Value Engineering program shall result in project savings of 5% to 15%, and a functional design constructible within budget. Utilizing a systematic approach, which identifies and provides the required functions of a project at the lowest possible cost, keeping the design intent unchanged, which shall result in the following:
•Savings on construction costs
•Lower life cycle costs
•Improved operational performance
 •Reduced maintenance costs
•Identification of risks and mitigation strategies
